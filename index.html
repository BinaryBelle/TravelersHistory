A Brief History of Travelers

Travelers has an extensive history as one of the oldest Insurance and Claim companies in the United States.

1864

Travelers was founded by a stonecutter named James Batterson.

1897

Travelers issues its first automobile insurance policy.

1968

Travelers developes the "CAT Van", a mobile insurance claim office designed to be used to aid policyholders in the event of a disaster.

2007

Travelers becomes the title sponser for the Travelers Championship.

2018

Travelers continues to be innovaters in the insurance industry and provides a variety of insurance options to its many satisfied customers.
